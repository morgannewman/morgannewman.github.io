Enter the Contest

An opportunity to be heard.

The TEDxUCSB team is looking for 3 UCSB students with ideas they'd like to share in an 8-12 minute TED talk at our event this year on Saturday, March 3, 2018.

Our theme this year is "Lost and Found." Interested applicants need to submit a 3-4 minute video in English, detailing what they want to share with the world. Don't worry, we aren't judging you on how nice your video looks.

The 3 contest winners will have the opportunity to be coached by our team to give an incredible talk at this year's TEDxUCSB event, which will be featured all over campus and distributed online.

Think you have good ideas? We'd like to hear them.
